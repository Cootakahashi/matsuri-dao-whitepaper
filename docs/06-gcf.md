# 🤝 GCF — the international community at the heart of co-creation

> From "audience" to "co-creator."

Matsuri Coin's vision of "from competition to co-creation" cannot be realized by technology and tokens alone.
Passionate people scattered around the world, connecting culture and people in their own locations, giving the ecosystem a pulse — that is **GCF (Global Community Friends).**

GCF is an **invitation-only community** involved in operating the Matsuri ecosystem.
Members organize cultural experiences in their own regions and grow the ecosystem from the ground up.
