## Risks relating to MTC

Acquiring, holding, and using MTC carries risks, including but not limited to the following.

### Price volatility risk
The price of MTC fluctuates with market supply and demand and **may fall significantly below the acquisition price.** In the worst case, the value may become zero. The economic mechanisms described in this whitepaper (flywheel, buyback, and so on) describe design structures — **they do not guarantee any price appreciation.**

### Liquidity risk
MTC is currently tradable on Raydium (DEX). If liquidity is insufficient, **you may be unable to sell at the desired price or timing.**

### Technical risk
- Smart contracts may contain bugs or vulnerabilities
- Failures or outages of the Solana network itself may make transactions temporarily impossible
- Data inconsistencies may arise during the off-chain → on-chain migration

### Regulatory risk
Crypto regulation is changing rapidly in every country. Future regulatory changes may **restrict or prohibit** the trading, use, or holding of MTC.

### Project risk
- Features and schedules described in this whitepaper may change
- The project may not proceed as planned, or may be discontinued
- Changes in the development team's structure may affect the roadmap

### Private key / wallet risk
If you lose your wallet's private key or seed phrase, **there is no way to recover your MTC.** We do not store users' private keys and cannot assist with recovery.
