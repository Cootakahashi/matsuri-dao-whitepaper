# 🤝 GCF — the international community at the heart of co-creation

> **From "audience" to "co-creator."**
> Joining GCF means becoming a companion in growing the Matsuri economy.

Matsuri Coin's vision of "from competition to co-creation" cannot be realized by technology and tokens alone.
Passionate people scattered across the world, connecting culture and people in their own locations, giving the ecosystem a pulse — and the core of that is **GCF (Global Community Friends).**

GCF is not a simple "membership."
It is a **business community** that shares revenue, and a **strictly invitation-only** partner organization (DAO) that participates in the very growth of the Matsuri ecosystem.

## Benefit 1: active mining (mining rights)

The **550 million MTC (about 61% of total supply)** that unlock on June 1, 2027 will not be dumped on the market. They are reserved as the **reward pool for contributors.**

:::tip Fully performance-based
Based on your activity (revenue, visitors brought in, guide sessions), MTC is paid out automatically from the pool.
Rewarded fairly, in proportion to what you did — that is GCF's reward design.
:::

### Rewards before the mining pool unlocks (2026 – May 2027)

MTC releases from the mining pool begin in June 2027, but **rewards are paid in the meantime as well:**

| Reward type | Content | Payment method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Referral commission** | Referral rewards for memberships, tours, events, etc. | USDC + MTC (instant) |
| **Guide rewards** | Per-event commission + tips | Bank transfer / crypto |
| **Contribution score accumulation** | All activity is recorded off-chain, and when the pool unlocks in June 2027, **MTC is distributed in bulk according to accumulated score** | Converted in June 2027 |

:::info In other words — the earlier you start, the better
Until June 2027 is a period for accumulating score. The members who are most active during this period will receive the largest MTC payout when the pool unlocks. Referral commissions and guide rewards are paid in real time, so **there is no period with zero income.**
:::

See [Tokenomics](/docs/tokenomics) for the detailed halving schedule.

:::tip Designed to reward early contributors more
As with Bitcoin, the halving makes **early releases the largest.** Those who are active in period 1 (2027–2029) will receive the most MTC. Rewards are determined not by "how early you joined" but by **"how much and how well you contributed."**
:::

## Benefit 2: high-value referral rewards

By referring high-value products (memberships, VIP tours, partner real estate, and so on), you earn **high-value rewards (USDC + MTC)** on a different scale from ordinary affiliate programs.
Rewards are **paid back instantly** via smart contract — no human in the middle, no delays, no opacity.

Referrals are not a one-off payout. As long as the people you referred continue to be active in the ecosystem, **continuous revenue sharing** keeps flowing.
In other words, the network you built becomes your own asset.

## The GCF role — what members do

GCF members are not "hold and wait" investors.
They are co-creators who move on the **ground** of the ecosystem and generate value.

| Activity | Content | Connected product |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **🗺️ Inviting guides** | Discover local culture guides and invite them onto the Matsuri platform | [Matsuri App](/docs/ecosystem) |
| **🎪 Planning events** | Plan, list, and attract guests for original culture-experience events and tours | [Matsuri App](/docs/ecosystem) |
| **📢 Publishing content** | Produce and spread J-Times articles and podcasts, bringing in new users | [J-Times](/docs/ecosystem) |
| **🤝 Community operations** | Grow local and online communities; deepen connections between members | GCF admin web |
| **📊 Operations** | Track referrals, analyze sales, and manage your team from the GCF admin dashboard | GCF admin web |

Every one of these activities is recorded on-chain as **Proof of Action**, directly tied to mining rewards.
"Doing what you love" becomes economic activity itself — this is Matsuri's co-creation model.
